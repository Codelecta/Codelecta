Why Learn PHP?

  • Easy to learn - simple syntax and beginner-friendly documentation.
  • Web-focused - specifically designed for web development and server-side scripting.
  • Database integration - excellent support for MySQL, PostgreSQL, and other databases.
  • Large community - extensive frameworks like Laravel, Symfony, and CodeIgniter.
  • High demand - powers over 75% of websites with server-side languages.
  • Open source and free - no licensing costs and continuous community development.
