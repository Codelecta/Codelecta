Why Learn Java?

  • Platform independent - runs on any device with JVM installed.
  • Extensive ecosystem with frameworks like Spring, Hibernate, and Apache Maven.
  • Strong memory management and automatic garbage collection.
  • Used by major companies like Oracle, Google, Netflix, and Amazon.
  • High demand in enterprise software development, Android development, and web services.
  • Strongly typed language that helps catch errors at compile time.
